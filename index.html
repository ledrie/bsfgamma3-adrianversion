    Vision

    We Dream Of Filipinos Who pasionately love their country and who's values and competencies enable them to realize their full potencial and contribute meaningfully to building the nation.As a learner centered public institution the department of education continuosly improves itself to better serve it's stakeholders.

    Mission

    To protect and promote the right of every Filipino to quality,equitable,culture-based and completed basic education where: students learned in a child friendly,gender-sensitive,safe and motivating environment Teachers facilitate learning and constantly nurture every learner Administrator and staff,as stewards of the institution,ensure an enabling and supportive environment for effective learning to happen family,community and other stakeholders are actively engaged andshare reponsibility for developing lifelong learners.
